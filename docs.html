Overview

Sneak-Up is a browser-based 2v2 tactical shooter with fog of war. Two teams of two spawn in opposite corners of a map and fight to eliminate each other. Vision is limited to line-of-sight - you can only see what your character can directly see.

The game runs on Colyseus for real-time multiplayer state sync, Express for the web server, and Firebase for authentication. All game logic runs on the server; the client only renders and sends input.

Quick Start

1. Create an account

Head to https://sneak-up-92152744325.europe-west1.run.app/ and sign in with Google or create an account with email and password. No email verification required.

2. Choose a mode

From the dashboard, select Play to enter a quickplay or ranked match, Practice to explore a map solo, or Bot 2v2 to play against AI.

3. Pick a map

Select from the available maps. Each has a distinct layout - open arenas, narrow corridors, or maze-like structures. You'll spawn in a corner with your teammate.

Controls

Three control modes are available, configurable from the Options panel in the dashboard.

Simple Tank

W/S - move forward/back  ·  A/D - rotate  ·  Space - shoot. Direction of fire follows your heading.

Tank + Mouse

Move with W/A/S/D while aiming independently with the mouse cursor. Left click to fire.

Gamepad

Left stick to move, right stick to aim. RT / R2 to shoot. Plug in any standard gamepad - it's detected automatically.

Game Modes

2v2 Quickplay

Unranked match against real players. No ELO impact. Good for learning maps and warming up.

Ranked 2v2

Competitive mode with ELO tracking. Win to gain points, lose to drop. Separate from the bot ladder.

Bot 2v2

Play with and against AI opponents. Bring your own bot brain or use the defaults. Useful for testing custom scripts or trained models.

Practice

Solo offline arena. No opponents, no timer - just the map. Good for learning layouts and controls.

Ranked & ELO

Sneak-Up uses a standard ELO system. Human players and bot players maintain separate ladders. All players start at 1200.

Replays

All matches are recorded automatically. Access your replays from the dashboard. Full playback controls: play, pause, rewind, and frame-by-frame scrubbing.
